Chunking is splitting long documents into smaller pieces so retrieval can find the most relevant parts.

Why overlap helps:
- Important information can sit on the boundary between two chunks.
- Overlap reduces the chance that the key sentence gets split away from its context.

Common chunking strategies:
- Fixed token/window size with overlap
- Split by headings, paragraphs, or semantic boundaries

A practical heuristic:
- Start with ~300-800 tokens per chunk
- 10% to 20% overlap
