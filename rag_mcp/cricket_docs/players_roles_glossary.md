# Players & Roles — Glossary (Learning Dataset)

## Batter
Primary role: score runs.

## Bowler
Primary role: take wickets and limit runs.

## All-rounder
Contributes significantly with both bat and ball.

## Wicket-keeper
A specialist fielder who stands behind the stumps and can:
- Take catches
- Perform stumpings

## Captain
Leads on-field strategy including field placements and bowling changes.

## Common bowling types
- Fast / pace
- Swing
- Seam
- Spin

## Types of spin (high level)
- Off-spin
- Leg-spin
